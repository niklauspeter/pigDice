HOW TO PLAY

This is a game of dice whereby two players play by rolling dice virtually, getting awarded random scores and trying to get to a final score of 100

The rules include:

  • when the player rolls and gets either 2-6, the score is cummulated and added to their total and they continue to roll
  • if a player gets a 1, on rolling, their total in that round reverts to 0 and the gameplay shifts to the next player
  • if a player chooses to hold, the total of that round is added to the final score and the gameplay shifts back to the next player
  • the first player to score 100 or above wins the game
